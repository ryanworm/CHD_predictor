The boring stuff...

Our machine learning algorithm used the data collected from the Framingham Heart Study. The Framingham Heart Study is a long term, on-going research study that led to much of our current knowledge the impacts of lifestyle on cardiovascular health.

The dataset utlized publically availible data from kaggle and was processed omitting any null values as well as overlapping attributes (eg. glucose levels and diabetes). Processing the data in this manner led to ~15% data loss leaving us with a dataset of n=3658. This was deemed sufficient for our non-production purposes.

To ensure reliable training of our machine learning algorithm, we upsampled our "CHD positive" samples, resulting in a dataset of n=6202.
