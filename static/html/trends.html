Google Trends

Calculating the MHI coefficient

Aggregate Google Trends data was taken for keyword searches highly correlated to numerous stressors. To get an aggregate score, monthly Google search volume was taken from each month and compared against the previous year. Once search lag was accounted for the monthly aggregate coefficient was multiplied by the baseline determined from the Statistics Canada dataset.
