Health Intake

This health intake form allows you to see your estimated risk level for coronary heart disease over the next ten years. Input information to the best of your knowledge. Labratory test results may be required for some fields, if unknown use normative value (NV).

This is application is designed for educational and training purposes only. This is in no way intended to provide a diagnosis and should not be used as part of any healthcare decision.

Enter your systolic blood pressure (one sig-digit), NV:120
Enter your total Cholesterol (mg/dL), NV:200
Enter your blood glucose level (mg/dL), NV:160
Enter your BMI ( height (m) / mass2 (kg) ), NV:22
Enter your resting heart rate (beats/min), NV:70
