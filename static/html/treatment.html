Treatment

Treatment and prevention of CHD are quite similar, treatment may involve more invasive procedues. Management is based primarily on diet, activity and lifestyle invertentions.

Lifestyle changes, such as eating a healthier (lower sodium, lower fat) diet, increasing physical activity, reaching a healthy weight, and quitting smoking. Medication to treat risk factors for CHD, such as high cholesterol, high blood pressure, or an irregular heartbeat may also be prescribed. In more severe cases surgical intervention may be required to help restore blood flow to the heart.

Education as to the importance of healthy living and importance of medicine as prescribed is also crucial. In many cases counseling for stress management or just improving mental health can help patients adhering to their treatment plans and have a dramatic role in recovery.
