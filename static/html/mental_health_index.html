Mental Health Index

Mental health is a growing concern as lockdown measures are being extended world-wide. Google Trends enables a near-real-time (NRT) estimate of the mental health index of regional population groups. Relative interest from Google Trends data has been shown to have statistically significant correlation to deaths by alcohol, drugs and suicide. This mental health index is an baseline estimate of the number of individuals (12 years of age and older) who are either seeking councilling or admit to suicidal ideation. Baseline data was take from Statistics Canada and modified with an aggregate coefficient calculated by year over year monthly Google search volume.
